Hezekiah's Shortcomings and Accomplishments

24 In those days Hezekiah was stricken with a terminal illness.40tn Heb “was sick to the point of dying.”
 He prayed to the Lord, who answered him and gave him a sign confirming that he would be healed.41tn Heb “and he spoke to him and a sign he gave to him.”
 25 But Hezekiah was ungrateful; he had a proud attitude, provoking God to be angry at him, as well as Judah and Jerusalem.42tn Heb “but not according to the benefit [given] to him did Hezekiah repay, for his heart was high, and there was anger against him and against Judah and Jerusalem.”
