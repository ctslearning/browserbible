The Southern Border of the Land

 the Lord spoke to Moses: 2 “Give these instructions2tn Or “command.”
 to the Israelites, and tell them: ‘When you enter Canaan, the land that has been assigned to you as an inheritance,3tn Heb “this is the land that will fall to you as an inheritance.”
 the land of Canaan with its borders, 3 your southern border4tn The expression refers to the corner or extremity of the Negev, the South.
 will extend from the wilderness of Zin along the Edomite border, and your southern border will run eastward to the extremity of the Salt Sea, 4 and then the border will turn from the south to the Scorpion Ascent,5tn Or “the Ascent of Scorpions” (עַקְרַבִּים [’aqrabbim] means “scorpions” in Hebrew).

The Western Border of the Land

 This will be your western border.

The Northern Border of the Land

7 “‘And this will be your northern border: From the Great Sea you will draw a line to Mount Hor; 8 from Mount Hor you will draw a line to Lebo Hamath,10tn Or “to the entrance to Hamath.”

The Eastern Border of the Land

10 “‘For your eastern border you will draw a line from Hazar Enan to Shepham. 11 The border will run down from Shepham to Riblah, on the east side of Ain, and the border will descend and reach the eastern side of the Sea of Chinnereth.11tn Or “the Sea of Galilee” (so NLT); NCV, TEV, CEV “Lake Galilee.”

Appointed Officials

16 The Lord said to Moses: 17 “These are the names of the men who are to allocate the land to you as an inheritance:16tn The verb can be translated simply as “divide,” but it has more the idea of allocate as an inheritance, the related noun being “inheritance.”
 Eleazar the priest and Joshua son of Nun. 18 You must take one leader from every17tn This sense is created by repetition: “one leader, one leader from the tribe.”
 tribe to assist in allocating the land as an inheritance.18tn The sentence simply uses לִנְחֹל (linkhol, “to divide, apportion”). It has been taken already to mean “allocate as an inheritance.” Here “assist” may be added since Joshua and Eleazar had the primary work.
 19 These are the names of the men: from the tribe of Judah, Caleb son of Jephunneh; 20 from the tribe of the Simeonites, Shemuel son of Ammihud; 21 from the tribe of Benjamin, Elidad son of Kislon; 22 and from the tribe of the Danites, a leader, Bukki son of Jogli. 23 From the Josephites, Hanniel son of Ephod, a leader from the tribe of Manasseh; 24 from the tribe of the Ephraimites, a leader, Kemuel son of Shiphtan; 25 from the tribe of the Zebulunites, a leader, Elizaphan son of Parnach; 26 from the tribe of the Issacharites, a leader, Paltiel son of Azzan; 27 from the tribe of the Asherites, a leader, Ahihud son of Shelomi; 28 and from the tribe of the Naphtalites, a leader, Pedahel son of Ammihud.” 29 These are the ones whom the Lord commanded to divide up the inheritance among the Israelites in the land of Canaan.
