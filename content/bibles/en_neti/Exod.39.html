The Making of the Priestly Garments

1 From the blue, purple, and scarlet yarn they made woven garments for serving in the sanctuary; they made holy garments that were for Aaron, just as the Lord had commanded Moses.1sn This chapter also will be almost identical to the instructions given earlier, with a few changes along the way.

The Breastpiece of Decision
